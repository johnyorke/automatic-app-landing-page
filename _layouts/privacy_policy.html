This app does not collect any personal data from the user. If you have opted into analytics on your iOS device, some anonomised usage statistics will be sent to Apple (go here for more details).

  • There are no 3rd party analytics (or otherwise) libraries built into the app
  • The health data that the app analyses never leaves your device
  • I, the app developer, cannot see any of your health data, anonomised or not
